The Application does not require user registration or an account. The Application may collect certain information automatically when you use it, including:

The Application accesses your device's photo library solely for the purpose of selecting and processing images for panorama splitting. Your photos are processed entirely on your device. No images are uploaded to any server or shared with any third party. Cropped images are saved locally to your device's photo library at your request.

All application data (crop history, settings, usage counters) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No personal data is transmitted to external servers.
